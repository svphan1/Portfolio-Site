Linguage

In Progress.
Linguage is a website that aims to solve a major problem for language learners, which is the difficulty in finding time to practice speaking. With Linguage, learners can easily practice by simply selecting the language they wish to practice, the language they speak, and then broadcasting it live to the language channels. Other learners who wants to trade the language specified can simply accept the request to be instantly conencted via peer-to-peer video chat. Next to the video chat will be a chat box with the ability to type and correct each other's grammar.

Build: React, JavaScript, Material UI, CSS3, Node.js, Python, WebRTC, PostgresSQL, Express.js, and Knex.js.

My Passport

Travel app that allows users to plan trips with friends. Upon logging in to the app, users view their dashboard - a google map that has their saved travel destinations pinned. Upon clicking the map, the user drops a pin to mark a new location they'd like to travel to. Users can also view the locations that other friends have pinned, allowing them to coordinate travel plans.

Build: Angular 7, JavaScript, CSS3, Bootstrap, Google Maps API, Node.js, PostgresSQL, Express.js, and Knex.js

Demo:   My Passport
